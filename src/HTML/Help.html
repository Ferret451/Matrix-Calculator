Help

If you're new to the program, here are some guidelines on how to work with different areas:

1. Types of operations

You have four windows to choose from: Expression, Determinant, Inverse, and Rank. To switch between them, there are corresponding buttons in the bottom left corner:

1.1 Expression



Here you can enter various expressions by combining matrices and regular numbers. For instructions on how to enter matrices, refer to section (2). (Unary operations are not available in this version, and for the reason why, please refer to the "About the Program" section =) )

1.2 Determinant

Here you can calculate the determinant for real matrices. By clicking on the plus sign, you increase the matrix dimension by 1 in height and 1 in width. By clicking on the minus sign, you decrease the dimension accordingly (both width and height change because the determinant can only be calculated for a square matrix). The min dimension is 1, the max - 100.

1.3 Inverse

Here you can calculate the determinant for real matrices. The plus-minus system is the same to determinate.

1.4 Rank

Here you can calculate rang of real matrix. If you want to change lines or columns amount, you need to input values in the text boxes (first - lines, second - columns). Min lines and columns amount - 1. Max - 100.
